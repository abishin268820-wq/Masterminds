# Store Policies - MasterMinds Snooker Equipment

## 1. Return and Refund Policy
At MasterMinds, we want you to be fully satisfied with your snooker equipment. If you are not happy with your purchase, we are here to help.

*   **Return Window:** You have **14 calendar days** to return an item from the date you received it.
*   **Conditions for Returns:** 
    *   The item must be unused and in the same condition that you received it.
    *   Your item must be in the original packaging.
    *   You must have the receipt or proof of purchase.
*   **Refund Eligibility:** Once we receive your item, we will inspect it and notify you that we have received your returned item. We will immediately notify you of the status of your refund after inspecting the item.
*   **Non-Returnable Items:**
    *   Used cue tips, chalk, or gloves (due to hygiene and wear).
    *   Custom-made or personalized snooker cues and tables.
    *   Items marked as "Final Sale."
*   **Refund Processing Time:** If your return is approved, we will initiate a refund to your original method of payment. You will receive the credit within **5–10 business days**, depending on your card issuer's policies.

---

## 2. Replacement Policy
If your product arrives damaged or defective, we will replace it at no additional cost to you.

*   **Process:** Contact our support team within **48 hours** of delivery to report damage or defects.
*   **Required Proof:** You must provide clear **photos or videos** of the damaged product and the shipping box.
*   **Replacement Time Frame:** Once the defect is verified, a replacement will be shipped within **3-5 business days**, subject to inventory availability.

---

## 3. Shipping and Delivery Policy
We strive to get your snooker gear to you as quickly and safely as possible.

*   **Order Processing Time:** All orders are processed within **1–2 business days**. Orders are not shipped or delivered on weekends or holidays.
*   **Shipping Methods:** We use reputable couriers (DHL, FedEx, UPS) for standard and express shipping. Snooker tables are shipped via specialized freight carriers.
*   **Estimated Delivery Timelines:**
    *   Standard Shipping: 5–7 business days.
    *   Express Shipping: 2–3 business days.
    *   Table Delivery: 10–20 business days (requires scheduling).
*   **Shipping Charges:** Shipping charges for your order will be calculated and displayed at checkout.
*   **Delays:** Factors like customs clearance or local logistics may cause delays. We will communicate any significant delays via email.

---

## 4. Cancellation Policy
*   **Pre-Shipment:** You can cancel your order for a full refund at any time before it has been processed for shipping.
*   **Post-Shipment:** Once an order has been shipped, it cannot be cancelled. You must follow the Return and Refund Policy once the item arrives.
*   **Custom Orders:** Cancellations for custom cues or tables are only accepted within **24 hours** of order placement.

---

## 5. Warranty Policy
We stand behind the quality of our equipment.

*   **Snooker Cues:** 6-month warranty against manufacturing defects (warping, joint issues). Does not cover cue tip wear or accidental breakage.
*   **Snooker Tables:** 1-year limited warranty on the slate and frame structural integrity.
*   **General Equipment:** 90-day warranty against factory defects.
*   **Manufacturer vs Store:** Some products may carry additional manufacturer-specific warranties. MasterMinds will facilitate claims with manufacturers on your behalf.

## 9. Damaged or Lost Shipment Policy
In the rare event that your gear is lost or damaged in transit:

*   **Lost Shipments:** If a shipment is marked "delivered" but not received, or has no tracking updates for 7+ days, contact us. We will open an investigation with the courier.
*   **Resolution:** If the courier confirms the shipment is lost, we will provide a full refund or send a one-time free replacement.
*   **Damaged Transit:** If the outer box is visibly crushed or soaked, please take photos **before** opening.

## 10. Inventory and Availability Policy
*   **Out-of-Stock Items:** If an item you ordered is out of stock, we will contact you within 24 hours to offer a full refund or an estimated backorder date.
*   **Order Fulfillment:** We fulfill orders on a "first-come, first-served" basis. Placing an item in your cart does not reserve the inventory until the checkout is complete.
